Almost 20 years of fighting to preserve my vision

In 2006, I received a diagnosis that shaped many years of my life. Since then I have gone through examinations, cross-linking, retinal laser treatment, consultations and a continuous search for a way to preserve my vision. I am now seeking treatment options in Israel and publishing my medical documents and fundraising progress openly.

Why this website exists

This website is not built on exaggeration. Its purpose is to show the facts: diagnoses, examinations, treatment procedures and medical recommendations over the years.

It brings together documents confirming my treatment journey, from the earliest examinations and cross-linking to retinal laser treatment and recent medical reports.

Key diagnoses

  • Pellucid Marginal Degeneration (PMD)
  • PMD grade I in the right eye
  • PMD grade IV in the left eye
  • High myopia in both eyes
  • Status after cross-linking and retinal laser treatment
